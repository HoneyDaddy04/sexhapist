Common questions

The things you were going to ask.

Is this actually private?

Yes. No name required. Conversations aren't tied to your real identity. We never share, sell, or pass your data to anyone.

Is the Sexhapist a licensed therapist?

No. Sexhapist is an AI-powered intimacy companion built to give you a private space to talk and learn. For licensed clinical care, we'll point you to qualified professionals when it makes sense.

Can my partner and I use it together?

Absolutely. Many men use it solo first, then bring their partner in for guided conversations and exercises that build connection.

Will it tell me to leave him?

No. We're here to help you understand him and yourself, not to push you toward any decision. If something is genuinely unsafe (abuse, manipulation), we'll surface real resources. Otherwise, the goal is connection.

Does he have to know I'm using it?

No. This is your space. Many women use it privately to think things through before bringing them up. Some share later. Both are valid.

What if what I am dealing with is heavy?

If something serious comes up, like trauma, crisis, or a medical concern, your Sexhapist will treat it with care and connect you to proper human support and resources.

How much does it cost?

Your first conversation is free. Pricing for full access will be shared in your account. No insurance required.
